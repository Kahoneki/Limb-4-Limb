Welcome To Limb For Limb. The epic fighting game where you can lose your robot limbs, but attacking with them makes you stronger!


P1 Controls:

WASD - Movement

R - Jab
T - Uppercut
F - Kick
G -Sweep


P2 Controls:

IJKL - Movement

9 - Jab
0 - Uppercut
O - Kick
P - Sweep


Much like in life, get your opponents health to 0 to win!
